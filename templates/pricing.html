Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free plan really free?

Yes — no credit card required. You get 2 scans per month forever on the free plan.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your billing portal anytime. You keep access until the end of the billing period.

What payment methods do you accept?

All major credit/debit cards via Stripe (Visa, Mastercard, Amex). Invoiced billing available for Enterprise.

Do I own my scan data?

Yes. Your scan results belong to you. We never share your data with third parties.

What is the monthly scan limit?

Scan counts reset on the 1st of each month. Unused scans do not roll over.
