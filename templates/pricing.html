FAQ

Common questions

Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. Cancel from your billing dashboard at any time. You keep access until the end of the current billing period — no refunds for partial months.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept M-Pesa (Vodacom), Airtel Money, Tigo Pesa, Visa/Mastercard, and bank transfer — processed securely by PesaPal. Your payment details never touch MulikaScans servers.
What happens when I hit my scan limit?
You'll see a clear message and a link to upgrade. Your existing scan history and results are never deleted. Scan counts reset on the 1st of each month.
Can I change plans?
Yes, upgrade or downgrade anytime from your billing dashboard. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the next billing date.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?
The Free plan is your trial — use it as long as you need. No credit card required to sign up.
Do you offer refunds?
We offer refunds within 7 days of first payment if you are unsatisfied. Contact support@mulikascans.com with your transaction reference.
